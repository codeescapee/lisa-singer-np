Welcome! I'm Lisa Singer, a board-certified Psychiatric Mental Health Nurse Practitioner (PMHNP-BC) specializing in mental health and psychiatric care.

At Singer Psych, my approach focuses on personalized, evidence-based treatment for conditions including ADHD, anxiety, depression, and other mental health concerns. I believe in treating the whole person with compassion and understanding.
